PokeQuiz aims to be the go to destination for Pokemon related quizzes! It features quizzes spanning the major corners of the Pokemon games, including Pokemon themselves, moves, and cries! You will play a continuous set of rounds, filling as much information as you can. At the end, you can either play again or view a breakdown of how well you did in each category! PokeQuiz also features a quiz generator, capable of creating fully customized quizzes about Pokemon, moves, and abilities! For more information, check out the info cards below. Click on one to begin playing!

Pokemon

A quiz all about a Pokemon's attributes. Be prepared to know a Pokemon's types, generation, abilities, base stat total, height, and weight!

Moves

A quiz all about a move's attributes. Given the description, be prepared to know a move's name, type, generation, PP, power, category, and accuracy!

Cries

A quiz about Pokemon cries. Put your listening ears on and fill in the Pokemon name based on its cry!

Sprites

A simple quiz about Pokemon names. Guess the Pokemon based on its in-game sprite!

Quiz Maker

A generator that builds fully customizable pokemon quizzes. Supports questions about Pokemon, Moves, and Abilities.

Quiz Player

Play your generated quizzes here! Supports quizzes created with the Quiz Maker.
